About

Our system involves a complex, machine learning algorithm which assesses the student against the OLNA marking guide to predict their grade. Our predictions have been estimated using a database of questions and responses from OLNA experienced teachers, and real students. Over time, our algorithm will get increasingly more accurate so that students will be ready to tackle the OLNA with confidence.
